About us

David Pilato
David Pilato is a Software Architect, Project Manager and Senior Developer keen on research and NoSQL Technologies and particularly on ElasticSearch.
He leads the french ElasticSearch community and gave speech on Devoxx France in 2012.
He implements plugins for the ElastiSearch ecosystem (a RSS River and Spring Factories).
He also implements a search solution based on ElasticSearch for one of the French government administration.
Malloum Laya
Malloum Laya is a Software Architect, Project Manager and Senior Developer keen on the NoSQL ecosystem (BigCouch, CouchDB, CouchBase and MongoDB).
He answered the big data needs for one of the French government administration. (more than 100 millions documents).
The company
ScrutMyDocs is the creation from the perfect match between one search website specialist and one stock database specialist.
The project was simply born while taking quality time with friends in Cergy, France trying to "re-do" the world.
The founders want to answer a real simple need: "I would like to easily search and find my documents even though they are stocked in several places in my companyâ€™s intranet".
There is several ways to stock data; however there is now only one way to get to it!
ScrutMyDocs is here to simplify the access to your data wherever they are initially saved.
We have the ambition to help you by giving you access to a wide range of ways to stock your data.
